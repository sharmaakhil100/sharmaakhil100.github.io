Privacy Policy

Mosaïq creates photo mosaics locally on your device from images you choose. The app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Photos and Camera

Mosaïq asks for access to Photos so you can choose source images and save finished mosaics. It may ask for Camera access so you can capture a master image. Selected photos are processed on device and are not uploaded to a server by Mosaïq.

Analytics and Tracking

Mosaïq does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or user accounts.

Data Retention

Mosaïq does not retain copies of your images or generated mosaics outside of what you choose to save to your device or Photos library.
